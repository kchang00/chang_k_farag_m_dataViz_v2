Distribution of Medals by Gender

Is the distribution of genders equal? Stats say that men have won more medals than women in the past overall, as well as in individual categories of medals (gold, silver and bronze). That's really too bad, because both genders (as well as the genders not included in these graphs) are awesome sauce. People are awesome and should be equal. Hopefully we will see a more even distribution of medals in the future.
